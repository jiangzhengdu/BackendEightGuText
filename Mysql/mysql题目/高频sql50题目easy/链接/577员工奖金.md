577员工奖金
===

题目
---

选出所有 bonus < 1000 的员工的 name 及其 bonus。

Employee 表单

+-------+--------+-----------+--------+
| empId |  name  | supervisor| salary |
+-------+--------+-----------+--------+
|   1   | John   |  3        | 1000   |
|   2   | Dan    |  3        | 2000   |
|   3   | Brad   |  null     | 4000   |
|   4   | Thomas |  3        | 4000   |
+-------+--------+-----------+--------+
empId 是这张表单的主关键字

Bonus 表单

+-------+-------+
| empId | bonus |
+-------+-------+
| 2     | 500   |
| 4     | 2000  |
+-------+-------+
empId 是这张表单的主关键字

思路
---

简单的left join

代码
---

``` sql
SELECT name, bonus
FROM Employee LEFT JOIN Bonus
ON Employee.empId = Bonus.empId
WHERE bonus < 1000 OR bonus IS NULL;
```
